Dinner Detain

Dinner Detain is a visual novel made in Construct 3. I coded, designed, and wrote the majority of dialogue.
My teammate Lee did all of the art, came up with the original concept for gameplay, design, and characters, as well as making the music.
This game was made for the 2024 New York SkillsUSA Interactive Application competition, which we won second place in.

The Graveyard

The Graveyard is a game made in Bitsy.

Pong With Lasers

Pong with lasers is Pong but with a twist!
The player is able to shoot a laser that can hit the other player, or hit the ball.
